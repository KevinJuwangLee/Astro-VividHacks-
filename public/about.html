About Astro

Use Astro to view the visibility of the wonders of the night sky, wherever you are. Explore the possibilities of your next nighttime adventure with Astro.


With the use of an algorithm to determine the brightness of light pollution using only a satellite picture, Astro determines the visibility percentage and the nighttime thrill you will experience.


Not needing the use of astronomical jargon, users can easily determine the visibility of stars by the easy-to-understand percentage just by hovering over a location of their choosing on our selected maps.


Whether you are a long-time resident, tourist, or planning your next nightly attraction, feel free to use Astro as a guide.

Note: I've included both South Korea and the United States of America, as I've lived in both countries, and I've always wished to view an eyefull of the beautiful night sky.
